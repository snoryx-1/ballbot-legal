Privacy Policy for BallBot

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

BallBot ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Discord User ID – Used to track statistics and premium status.
  • Server (Guild) ID – Used for server-specific features and cooldowns.
  • Username (Display Only) – Shown in leaderboards. Not stored permanently.

1.2 Game Statistics

  • Voting choices and win rates
  • Guess career streaks and accuracy
  • Blind ranking submissions
  • Dream draft teams and ratings
  • Tournament participation
  • Command usage timestamps

1.3 Premium Subscription Data

  • Discord User ID
  • Subscription plan (monthly/yearly)
  • Activation & expiration date
  • Payment confirmation ID (no financial details)

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment card details
  • Passwords or authentication tokens
  • Direct message content
  • Voice or video data
  • Email addresses (unless voluntarily provided)
  • IP addresses, device information, or location data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process commands and features
  • Track leaderboards and statistics
  • Manage premium subscriptions
  • Improve and optimize performance
  • Respond to support requests

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 Database

Data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas using encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) and secure authentication.

3.2 Data Retention

  • Active users: Retained while bot is in use
  • Inactive premium: Deleted 30 days after expiration
  • Deletion requests: Processed within 30 days
  • Backups: Retained for 30 days

3.3 Security Measures

  • Password-protected database
  • Limited authorized access
  • Regular updates & monitoring

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We only share data with necessary service providers:

  • Discord, Inc. – Platform provider
  • MongoDB Atlas – Database hosting
  • Payment Processors – For premium subscriptions

We do NOT sell, market, or share data with advertisers.

5. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Stop using the bot at any time

To request deletion or access, contact us via our Discord servers listed below.

6. Children's Privacy

BallBot follows Discord’s 13+ age requirement and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. International Users

BallBot is operated from India. Data may be stored globally via MongoDB Atlas secure servers.

8. Cookies & Tracking

BallBot does not use cookies or browser tracking. Discord may use cookies on their platform.

9. Data Breaches

In case of a breach, affected users will be notified within 72 hours where required.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of BallBot constitutes acceptance of changes.
